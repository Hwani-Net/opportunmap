## Design System Strategy: The Precision Curator

### 1. Overview & Creative North Star

This design system is built to transform a high-utility calendar tool into a **"Precision Curator."** While many calendar applications feel like rigid spreadsheets, this system adopts a high-end editorial approach. We move beyond the "standard" functional aesthetic by leveraging intentional asymmetry, sophisticated tonal layering, and a focus on Korean-optimized typography.

The North Star is **Subtle Authority.** The UI should feel as organized as a Swiss timepiece but as breathable as a contemporary art gallery. We achieve this by rejecting the "boxed-in" layout of traditional calendars in favor of open, fluid spaces defined by light and tone rather than lines.

## Elevation & Depth: Tonal Layering

Traditional shadows and borders create "visual noise." This system uses **Tonal Stacking** and **Ambient Depth**.

| Technique | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Layering Principle** | Stack `surface-container-lowest` card on `surface-container-low` section |
| **Ghost Border** | `outline-variant` at 10-20% opacity — felt, not seen |
| **Ambient Shadows** | 32px blur, 4% opacity of `on-surface`. Soft morning cast, not digital drop shadow |
| **Glassmorphism** | `surface` at 80% opacity + `20px` backdrop-blur for top nav |

## Signature Layout: The Asymmetric Grid

- Sidebar: significantly narrower than detail panel, `surface-container-low` fill
- Month/Year header: offset to the left (Manrope Black)
- "Add Event" CTA: floats asymmetrically on the right, `surface-container-highest` elevation
- This "Editorial Offset" makes the app feel bespoke, not generic

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use white space as a structural element — increase `surface-container` contrast rather than adding lines
- Use `title-lg` for Korean titles
- Align all elements to the 4px base grid

### Don't
- Use `#000000` for text — use `on-surface` (`#131b2e`)
- Use heavy gradients — maximum: 2% vertical tint shift from `primary` to `primary-container`
- Use `xl` (12px) radius on small components like chips
